A few honest things

What can I actually make back from one product?

A well-positioned digital product priced between $9 and $35 only needs 10-30 sales to cover what you invested here — and digital products keep selling long after launch, with no extra production cost per sale.

I'm not a "guru" — is this for me?

This isn't a course on how to get rich. It's hands-on help turning what you already know into something real — using the same process behind my own product.

What if I don't have a finished idea yet?

Most people don't. That's what the clarity call is for — we figure out what your knowledge could become together, no pressure to arrive with anything polished.

What if I spend $100 and it doesn't feel right?

The clarity call happens first, for free — you'll already know what you're getting before any money changes hands. The deposit only happens once you've said yes to a clear plan.

What if my product doesn't sell?

That's a real possibility with any product, and it's not on you alone — once it's built, I'm happy to share simple tips for getting it in front of people, based on what's worked for my own audience.

How long does the whole thing take?

From clarity call to finished product is typically about a week. You'll know the timeline before you pay anything.
